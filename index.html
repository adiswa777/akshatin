About Akshat Institute of Management & Technology

🎓 Empowering Future Leaders Through Quality Education | Innovation | Excellence 🎓

Akshat Institute of Management & Technology is a forward-thinking educational institution dedicated to providing excellence in management, technology, and professional education.

The institute fosters a student-centric environment supported by experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and industry-oriented programs that bridge the gap between theory and real-world application.

With a strong focus on innovation, leadership, and skill development, Akshat Institute prepares students to become confident professionals and responsible citizens ready to excel in a competitive global landscape.

Akshat Institute of Management & Technology is a private computer training and vocational institute located in Azamgarh, Uttar Pradesh, mainly focused on providing practical, skill-based education rather than traditional degree programs. It offers a range of short-term and diploma courses such as CCC, DCA, ADCA, O Level (NIELIT), Tally with GST, typing (Hindi and English), and basic to advanced training in programming, web development, graphic design, and data analytics. The institute is known for its emphasis on hands-on learning, affordable fee structure, and personalized attention to students, making it a suitable option for beginners or those looking to build job-oriented computer skills for employment or government exam requirements. However, it is important to note that it is not a full-fledged college offering degrees like B.Tech or MBA, and it does not provide large-scale campus placement opportunities like major universities; instead, its primary goal is to enhance practical knowledge and improve employability through technical training.
